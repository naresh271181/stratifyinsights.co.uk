What we run for you

Your complete finance engine.
Live from day one.

Every service below runs continuously inside your business — connected to your systems, updated in real time, and delivered directly to your dashboard. No templates. No manual hand-offs. No waiting.

Management Accounts
We deliver fully reconciled management accounts—P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow—directly to your dashboard within five working days of month-end. This includes automated variance analysis against prior periods and budgets, complete with department-level breakdowns and executive summaries for board-level review.
Cash Flow & Runway Tracking
Move beyond static spreadsheets with a dynamic 13-week rolling cash flow forecast, updated continuously. We provide exact runway visibility, working capital analysis, and automated alerts for covenant thresholds, ensuring you never face a liquidity surprise.
Annual Budget & Plan
We construct a fully integrated three-statement annual plan engineered around your specific growth targets, hiring schedules, and capital requirements. This serves as your definitive financial baseline, allowing for precise tracking of revenue models and departmental costs throughout the fiscal year.
Scenario & What-If Modelling
De-risk your strategic decisions before capital is committed. We build dynamic financial models that allow you to instantly toggle between base, upside, and downside scenarios—revealing the integrated impact of a new hire, a lost contract, or a market shift on your bottom line.
KPI Dashboard
A live command centre engineered for your specific business model. We distil complex backend ledger data into pristine, real-time visual dashboards that track month-on-month trends, flash revenue visibility, and the core metrics demanded by your executive team and investors.
Financial Modelling
Secure capital and execute transactions with investor-grade financial architecture. We build bespoke, integrated models for fundraising rounds, M&A due diligence, unit economic pricing decisions, and debt capacity analysis, giving you absolute mathematical leverage in the boardroom.
Automated Bookkeeping
We maintain a perpetual, audit-ready ledger connected natively to your ERP or accounting software. By executing daily bank reconciliations, automated fixed asset depreciation, and continuous accrual tracking, we eliminate transaction backlogs and month-end panic entirely.
Accounts Payable — P2P
Transform your purchase-to-pay cycle from a manual bottleneck into an automated pipeline. We handle high-volume supplier invoice capture, statement reconciliations, and payment run scheduling, providing you with real-time aged creditor visibility without lifting a finger.
Accounts Receivable — O2C
Protect your cash conversion cycle with an order-to-cash engine running on autopilot. We manage invoice dispatch, automated debtor tracking, and precise cash allocation, ensuring your revenue is collected faster and your aged debtor metrics remain pristine.
Month-End Close
We execute a flawless, hard close within three working days of month-end. Our process includes comprehensive closing journals, complete balance sheet reconciliation packs, and inter-company matching, resulting in an audit-ready trial balance every single period.
Tax Compliance — VAT & GST
Your indirect tax obligations are mathematically protected and filed with zero delays. We calculate VAT, GST, and input tax apportionments directly from your live, reconciled transaction data, ensuring absolute compliance with digital tax mandates across your operating jurisdictions.
Year-End & Statutory
We eliminate the friction of annual reporting by delivering a complete, fully referenced year-end file. You receive the final trial balance, loan amortization schedules, and pristine working papers perfectly prepared for your external auditors or filing accountants.
