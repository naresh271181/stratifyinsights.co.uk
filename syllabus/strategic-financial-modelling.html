Strategic Financial Modelling

Commercialisation Layer 5 Weeks Cohort Live

Programme Overview

Financial modelling built for strategic decisions. This programme covers three-statement model construction, DCF valuation, scenario and sensitivity analysis, and translating complex financial outputs into board-level narratives that drive capital allocation decisions.

"A model that cannot be understood by the decision-maker is not a model — it is a liability. Learn to build both the numbers and the story."

What You'll Learn

  • Build integrated three-statement financial models from scratch
  • Apply DCF, comparable company, and precedent transaction valuation methods
  • Design scenario and sensitivity analyses for strategic investment decisions
  • Construct project finance and infrastructure funding models
  • Present financial models to boards and investment committees with confidence

Programme Modules

Module 01
Three-Statement Modelling
Building integrated P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow models with dynamic linkages and audit trails.
Module 02
DCF & Valuation
Discounted cash flow construction, WACC derivation, terminal value methods, and sensitivity to key assumptions.
Module 03
Scenario & Sensitivity Analysis
Scenario modelling frameworks, Monte Carlo basics, and building decision trees for strategic capital allocation.
Module 04
Project & Infrastructure Finance
Project finance structures, debt sculpting, DSCR modelling, and public sector and PPP funding architecture.
Module 05
Financial Storytelling
Communicating model outputs to non-technical audiences — board packs, investment memos, and executive presentations.

Who This Programme Is For

CFOs, finance directors, strategy leads, investment analysts, and senior practitioners responsible for capital allocation, M&A, or strategic planning in corporate, or institutional contexts.
