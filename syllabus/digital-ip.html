Commercialisation Layer

Digital IP Strategy

Commercialisation Layer 4 Weeks Self-paced Online

Programme Overview

Monetise digital intellectual property at enterprise scale. This programme covers patent strategy, licensing architecture, technology transfer frameworks, and building revenue models for digital and AI-generated assets across multiple jurisdictions.

"Most institutions create IP. Very few have a strategy to extract value from it. This programme changes that."

What You'll Learn

  • Identify, protect, and register digital intellectual property across key jurisdictions
  • Design licensing frameworks that generate recurring institutional revenue
  • Structure technology transfer agreements between universities, corporates, and public institutions
  • Navigate IP ownership for AI-generated and co-created digital assets
  • Build a commercial IP portfolio strategy aligned to organisational objectives

Programme Modules

Module 01
IP Foundations for Digital Assets
Patents, copyright, trade secrets, and trademarks in the digital and AI context — what you own and what you don't.
Module 02
Licensing Architecture
Exclusive vs non-exclusive models, royalty structures, SaaS IP licensing, and building recurring revenue from IP.
Module 03
Technology Transfer
University-industry transfer frameworks, spinout structures, public sector IP policy, and cross-border considerations.
Module 04
AI & Co-Created IP
Ownership of AI-generated outputs, collaborative IP agreements, and the emerging regulatory landscape for algorithmic assets.

Who This Programme Is For

General counsel, technology transfer officers, research directors, and senior commercial leads in universities, corporates, and public institutions with IP-generating activities.
