Open Innovation

Innovation Engine 5 Weeks Cohort Live

Programme Overview

Co-innovation frameworks for enterprise leaders. This programme covers building startup partnerships, hackathon design, open-source ecosystems, and accelerating value creation through outside-in digital capability acquisition — culminating in the innovation accounting frameworks needed to measure, report, and defend the value of everything you build.

"The best ideas rarely come from inside the building. Open Innovation is the discipline of finding them systematically — and Innovation Accounting is the discipline of proving their worth."

What You'll Learn

  • Design and run structured innovation challenges and hackathons
  • Build and manage startup partnership programmes within enterprise governance
  • Evaluate open-source ecosystem opportunities for strategic value capture
  • Create outside-in capability acquisition frameworks
  • Apply innovation accounting — OKRs, AARRR, and portfolio metrics — to measure and report innovation ROI
  • Build the board-level business case for sustained innovation investment

Programme Modules

Module 01
Foundations of Open Innovation
The Chesbrough model, inside-out vs outside-in flows, and why enterprise innovation fails without external input.
Module 02
Startup & Ecosystem Partnerships
Scouting frameworks, partnership structures, IP considerations, and running pilot-to-scale programmes.
Module 03
Hackathon & Challenge Design
Designing high-quality innovation challenges, judging frameworks, and converting outputs into real initiatives.
Module 04
Open Source Strategy
Evaluating open-source ecosystems, contributing vs consuming, and building institutional capability on open platforms.
Module 05
Innovation Accounting
Replacing vanity metrics with validated learning — OKR design, AARRR funnels, horizon 1/2/3 portfolio accounting, and building the board-level investment narrative for sustained innovation spend.

Who This Programme Is For

Innovation directors, R&D leaders, corporate venture teams, and innovation hub managers building structured external innovation programmes.
