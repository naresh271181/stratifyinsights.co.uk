Blockchain & DeFi

Tech Infrastructure 4 Weeks Self-paced Online

Programme Overview

Decentralised finance is restructuring the architecture of money. This programme gives finance and enterprise leaders a rigorous, practitioner-led grounding in blockchain infrastructure, DeFi protocols, tokenisation models, and digital asset regulation — without requiring a technical background.

"DeFi is not a technology experiment. It is a structural challenge to how institutions intermediate value — and finance leaders need to understand it before it redefines their role."

What You'll Learn

  • Evaluate blockchain architectures and their fit for institutional finance use cases
  • Understand DeFi protocols — AMMs, lending, staking, and yield mechanisms
  • Analyse tokenisation models for real-world assets, funds, and debt instruments
  • Read and interpret smart contract logic and assess audit considerations
  • Navigate digital asset regulation across UK FCA, UAE VARA, and US SEC/CFTC frameworks

Programme Modules

Module 01
Blockchain Architecture for Finance
Distributed ledger fundamentals, consensus mechanisms, public vs private chains, and why institutional blockchain decisions are trust architecture decisions, not technology ones.
Module 02
DeFi Protocols & Institutional Applications
How AMMs, decentralised lending, stablecoins, and yield protocols work — and where institutional treasury, payments, and settlement adoption is viable today.
Module 03
Tokenisation & Real-World Assets
Asset tokenisation frameworks for equities, real estate, fund units, and debt instruments — security token structures, custody, and primary/secondary market mechanics.
Module 04
Smart Contracts, Risk & Regulation
Smart contract architecture for non-engineers, audit and failure case studies, and the digital asset regulatory landscape across UK, UAE, and US jurisdictions.

Who This Programme Is For

Finance directors, treasury managers, legal counsel, investment analysts, and senior enterprise practitioners evaluating DeFi adoption, digital asset strategy, or advising institutions operating in tokenised markets.
