AI & Enterprise Governance

Governance & Trust 4 Weeks Cohort Live

Programme Overview

Enterprise AI governance built for decision-makers. This programme covers the EU AI Act, algorithmic accountability frameworks, AI risk classification, board-level oversight design, and constructing institutional trust architecture that enables responsible AI deployment at scale.

"Governance is not a constraint on AI adoption — it is the foundation that makes enterprise AI trustworthy enough to actually deploy."

What You'll Learn

  • Navigate the EU AI Act and its implications for enterprise AI deployment
  • Design an AI risk classification and tiering framework for your organisation
  • Build board-level AI oversight structures and accountability chains
  • Implement algorithmic audit and explainability processes
  • Align AI governance to ESG, data protection, and sector-specific regulatory requirements

Programme Modules

Module 01
The AI Governance Landscape
Global regulatory frameworks — EU AI Act, UK AI Safety Institute, US Executive Order — and what they require of enterprise leaders.
Module 02
Risk Classification & Tiering
AI system risk categories, prohibited uses, high-risk system requirements, and building your internal classification framework.
Module 03
Board Oversight & Accountability
Governance structures for AI — board committees, AI ethics officers, accountability chains, and reporting frameworks.
Module 04
Algorithmic Audit & Explainability
Model documentation, bias assessment, explainability standards, and building audit-ready AI systems for regulated sectors.

Who This Programme Is For

Board members, general counsel, chief risk officers, compliance directors, and senior enterprise leaders responsible for AI strategy and governance in regulated organisations.
