Innovation Accounting

Commercialisation Layer 2 Weeks Self-paced Online

Programme Overview

Traditional accounting frameworks were built for businesses that already know what they are making. Innovation requires a different set of metrics — ones that measure learning, not just output. This short course equips finance and innovation leaders with the accounting frameworks, OKR structures, and portfolio measurement tools needed to govern, fund, and communicate the value of innovation investment.

"Vanity metrics kill innovation investment. The finance function needs frameworks that measure validated progress — not activity."

What You'll Learn

  • Apply innovation accounting principles to replace vanity metrics with validated learning measures
  • Design OKR frameworks and AARRR funnels for innovation-stage initiatives
  • Build an innovation portfolio accounting model that balances horizon 1, 2, and 3 investments
  • Construct the board-level business case for sustained R&D and innovation spend
  • Integrate innovation KPIs into management reporting and CFO dashboards

Programme Modules

Module 01
Why Traditional Accounting Fails Innovation
The mismatch between conventional P&L thinking and innovation economics — and how lean startup, stage-gate, and venture-portfolio frameworks offer a better lens.
Module 02
Metrics That Matter: OKRs & AARRR
Designing OKRs for innovation initiatives, building AARRR acquisition-to-revenue funnels, and defining actionable metrics that drive decision-making rather than reporting.
Module 03
Innovation Portfolio Accounting
Horizon 1/2/3 portfolio structures, allocating capital across certainty and exploration, and tracking ROI across an innovation pipeline rather than individual projects.
Module 04
Board-Level Reporting & Investment Cases
Translating innovation metrics into board language, building the R&D investment narrative, and integrating innovation KPIs into CFO dashboards and quarterly reporting.

Who This Programme Is For

CFOs, FDs, finance business partners, innovation leads, and heads of strategy in corporates, scaleups, and public institutions responsible for measuring, funding, or governing innovation investment — particularly those bridging the finance function and innovation teams.

Designed to be completed in two focused weeks. Includes innovation accounting templates, OKR design frameworks, and a board reporting slide pack. Get in touch →
