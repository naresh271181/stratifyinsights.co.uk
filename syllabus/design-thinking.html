Design Thinking

Innovation Engine 3 Weeks Cohort Workshop

Programme Overview

Apply human-centred design methodology to enterprise problems. This programme equips practitioners with the full DT toolkit — empathy mapping, problem framing, rapid prototyping, and testing propositions with end users before build. Structured around real friction points inside large organisations.

"Human-centred design is not a process — it's a mindset. This programme makes it a practice."

What You'll Learn

  • Map user journeys and extract actionable insight from qualitative research
  • Frame complex organisational problems using HMW and POV techniques
  • Facilitate structured ideation sessions across cross-functional teams
  • Build and test low-fidelity prototypes with real stakeholders
  • Embed design thinking as a repeatable innovation process in your institution

Programme Modules

Module 01
Empathy & Discovery
User interviews, observation methods, empathy mapping, and synthesising insight from qualitative research.
Module 02
Problem Framing
How Might We statements, Point of View framing, and redefining the problem space before jumping to solutions.
Module 03
Ideation Methods
Brainstorming techniques, SCAMPER, worst possible idea, and structured divergent thinking facilitation.
Module 04
Prototyping & Testing
Low-fidelity prototyping, rapid testing cycles, feedback frameworks, and iterating based on real user response.
Module 05
Institutional Embedding
Making DT repeatable — sprint frameworks, playbooks, and building an innovation culture inside large organisations.

Who This Programme Is For

Strategy teams, innovation leads, product managers, and senior practitioners inside corporates, public sector agencies, and universities who need to solve complex problems with a structured, human-centred process.
