Product Management

Innovation Engine 6 Weeks Cohort Live

Programme Overview

End-to-end product management in enterprise environments. This programme covers roadmap strategy, stakeholder alignment, OKR frameworks, prioritisation models, and managing cross-functional delivery teams inside complex, regulated organisations.

"Enterprise PM is not about features — it's about making the right bets, aligning the right people, and shipping things that move the needle."

What You'll Learn

  • Build and communicate a product roadmap aligned to strategic OKRs
  • Apply prioritisation frameworks (RICE, MoSCoW, Kano) to manage backlog decisions
  • Navigate stakeholder complexity in matrixed enterprise organisations
  • Design discovery and delivery processes for large-scale regulated environments
  • Lead cross-functional teams through ambiguity and competing priorities
  • Scale from feature delivery to product-led growth at institutional level

Programme Modules

Module 01
Product Strategy & Vision
Defining product vision, aligning to business strategy, and translating OKRs into actionable roadmaps.
Module 02
Discovery & Validation
User research in enterprise contexts, hypothesis testing, and building the case for new product investment.
Module 03
Prioritisation & Backlog Management
RICE, MoSCoW, Kano models — choosing what to build and communicating the rationale to stakeholders.
Module 04
Stakeholder & Governance
Managing upwards, presenting to boards, and navigating procurement and compliance in large organisations.
Module 05
Delivery & Launch
Sprint planning, cross-functional team management, go-to-market coordination, and post-launch measurement.
Module 06
Scaling & Portfolio Management
Managing multiple products, platform thinking, and transitioning from feature delivery to product-led growth.

Who This Programme Is For

Product managers, programme directors, digital transformation leads, and senior practitioners in corporates, and universities building or scaling enterprise-grade digital products.
