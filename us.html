01 / JIB & AP
Managed JIB & Accounts Payable
Eliminate the Headcount Bottleneck.
Tracking, coding, and mathematically splitting thousands of monthly oilfield field tickets across complex joint-venture ownership decks is the single biggest operational bottleneck for independent operators. We deploy dedicated, secure squads directly into your ERP ecosystem to handle the volume seamlessly.
High-Volume Ingestion: Overnight field-ticket-to-invoice matching and line-item well coding metrics.
COPAS Compliance: Strict adherence to US joint-venture overhead rules, minimizing partner billing disputes.
AFE Tracking: Real-time monitoring of drilling and completion capital deployments against approved budgets.

02 / Revenue
Production Revenue Accounting
Zero Cash Leakage. Absolute Volumetric Precision.
Revenue accounting carries the highest financial risk in the upstream lifecycle. We leverage enterprise-trained precision to perfectly balance physical wellhead volumes against midstream purchaser cash receipts, actively protecting your top-line revenue.
Automated Ingestion & Netting: Deep expertise in processing multi-page purchaser check stubs via Enverus EnergyLink.
Volumetric-to-Value Reconciliations: Auditing physical field run-tickets against paid volumes to identify pipeline transport overcharges.
Prior Period Adjustments (PPAs): Flawless tracking of retroactive midstream volume adjustments and severance tax withholdings.

03 / FP&A
Strategic Energy FP&A
From Raw Transactional Data to Boardroom Strategy.
We do not just close the books — we elevate your clean transaction data into high-value executive intelligence. Because we manage both your expenses (JIB) and your income (Revenue), we build dynamic financial models without the standard 30-day reporting lag.
Well-Level Profitability: Custom Lease Operating Statements isolating lifting costs per Boe to identify underperforming acreage.
Dynamic Cash Flow Forecasting: Layering gas settlement data, operational forecasts, and hedging derivatives against forward curves.
Reserve & DD&A Valuation: Reconciling financial depreciation with engineering reserve reports for bank redeterminations or asset divestitures.
